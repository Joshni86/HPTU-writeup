# Sprawling Amusement Park

**Points**: 500  
**Solves**: 2  
**Flag Format**: HPTU{XX.xxXXxxX,-YY.yyYYyyY}

## Solution

1. **OSINT search** for a missing girl in an amusement park — likely a real or mock social media post with a geotagged image.
2. Identify the park using landmarks (e.g., Disney World’s Cinderella Castle, Epcot Spaceship Earth).
3. Use Google Earth to match the perspective in the image.
4. Zoom in to the exact spot (e.g., near a bench, entrance, or trash can) where the girl was last seen.
5. Obtain high-precision decimal degrees from Google Earth.
6. **Flag example:** `HPTU{28.3772445,-81.5707418}`
